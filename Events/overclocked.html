General Guidelines


  • Use of any unethical application will lead to immediate disqualification from the tournament.

  • The event would be held on 24th and 25th July from 4:00 pm onwards. Brackets and exact timings shall be shared on the Discord server.

  • ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA: Top 16 teams (on the basis of the rank) will be allowed to compete.
